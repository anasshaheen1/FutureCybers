How to Play

Welcome to FutureCybers! This game is designed to teach you important cybersecurity concepts through a series of challenges.

  1. Select a level and complete each challenge.
  2. Read the instructions carefully before attempting each challenge.
  3. You must complete each challenge to unlock the next one.
  4. Use the "Hint" button if you need help, but try to solve the challenge on your own first.
  5. Your progress will be saved, and you can return to completed challenges anytime.

Points System

Earn points as you progress through the challenges:

Challenge Level Points Per Challenge
Level 1 Challenges 10 points each
Level 2 Challenges 50 points each
Level 3 Challenges 100 points each

Your total score will be displayed at the top left corner of the game screen. Complete all challenges to achieve the highest score!

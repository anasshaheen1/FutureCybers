Phishing Email Explained

Common signs of phishing emails include:

  • Suspicious links or URLs.
  • Urgent or threatening language.
  • Request for sensitive information.
  • Poor grammar or spelling errors.

Always verify with the organization through official channels and avoid clicking on suspicious links.
